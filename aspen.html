Did you know that you can tell and Aspen by the way it is?

Compare an Aspen with other trees and their identification:

  1. Douglas Fir

    • Normally green
    • Used as Christmas Trees
    • Have farms devoted to their care and selling
    • The preferred Santa tree

  2. Oak

    • Spiky leaves that get stuck in your feet
    • Grow super big after a lot of time
    • Dark green
    • Treehouse trees

  3. Sequoia

    • Tallest trees I've ever seen
    • Can drive a car through them
    • In the Sequoia National Park
    • If a pinecone falls and hits your head, you're dead

  4. Aspen

    • The way it is
